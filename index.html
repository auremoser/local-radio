WWEN:
ENTERTIN INDIE RADIO

WWEN a fresh voice, on the air since February 29, 2016. We broadcast music you never hear on the normcore radio with LIVE, local DJs doing live shows. Plus, we offer a wide range of national and local news programming that cover issues underrepresented by other media. WWEN is a true community radio station.

About Us

WWEN plays cool indie and local music from Monday to Friday throughout Washburn County.

We are locally owned, and by charter can never be bought out by any non-local entity. The station is operated by volunteers from the Entertin community. Our staff consists of your neighbors playing music, sharing news and operating the station.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT RADIO

Our founders believed that great radio was a collaborative effort of great community, and WWEN has been proud to serve the tri-city area of Washburn County for several years.

We play a mixture of genres and show types, and function as a media projection hub for both the public access cable network and community radio.

Our Shows

Music at WWEN is an eclectic mix of contemporary indie, experimental country, electronic and instrumental magic that only a with the motto "Foisons Exeunt" could craft. Check out some examples of our shows below.
